The African Genomic Medicine Portal (AGMP) is a search platform which provides access to published gene and variant information as it relates to health and medicine. This info is specifically provided in the context of African populations, providing access to information derived from studying African populations indigenous to Africa and the related diaspora. This makes AGMP the first comprehensive resource for genomic medicine in Africa. AGMP integrates data curated from existing repositories related to health and medicine, including  PharmGKB and DisGeNET.

 

The current version of AGMP (v1.0) provides access to information related to 561 genes, 80 drugs, 416 diseases, and 2338 variants. Information can be accessed through the Search page.

 

AGMP can be employed for various research purposes including the investigation of the molecular underpinnings of human diseases and drug processing, text mining, and the generation of hypothesis related to human disease and drug processing.
